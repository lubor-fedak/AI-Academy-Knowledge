Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Visit the IT portal at https://it.kyndryl.com and click "Reset Password". You'll receive an email with instructions.

Q: Who do I contact for laptop issues?
A: Contact the IT helpdesk at helpdesk@kyndryl.com or call +420 123 456 789.

Q: How do I book a meeting room?
A: Use the Outlook calendar to find and book available rooms. Conference rooms can be booked up to 2 weeks in advance.

Q: What is the guest WiFi password?
A: Guest WiFi network is "Kyndryl-Guest". Password changes monthly and is posted in reception.

Q: How do I submit expenses?
A: Use the Concur app to submit expense reports. All receipts must be attached. Reports are processed within 5 business days.

Q: Where can I find the company org chart?
A: The org chart is available on the intranet under "Our Company" > "Organization".

Q: How do I request time off?
A: Submit leave requests through Workday at least 2 weeks before the planned absence.
