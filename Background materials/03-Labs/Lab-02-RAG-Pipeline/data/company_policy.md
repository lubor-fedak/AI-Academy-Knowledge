# Kyndryl Employee Handbook (Sample)

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible working arrangements are available with manager approval.

### Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work days must be coordinated with your team to ensure adequate office coverage.

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All employees receive 25 days of annual leave per year
- Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval is required for all leave requests

### Sick Leave
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave does not carry over to the next year

## Expense Policy

### Travel Expenses
- Economy class flights for trips under 6 hours
- Business class allowed for trips over 6 hours
- Hotel expenses capped at €150 per night within Europe
- Meal allowance: €50 per day

### Equipment
- Standard laptop provided to all employees
- Additional equipment requires manager approval
- Personal phone usage reimbursed up to €50/month
