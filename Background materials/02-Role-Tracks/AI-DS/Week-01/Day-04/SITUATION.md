# Day 4: How Good Is Good?

## AI-DS Track - Week 1, Day 4

### The Situation: "Is Our AI Working?"

PM asks: "How good is our AI assistant?"
You answer: "Pretty good."
PM: "Can you be more specific?"

**Challenge:** Define measurable quality metrics for an AI system.

### AI Quality Dimensions

| Dimension | Metric | How to Measure |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Accuracy | Answer correctness | Human evaluation |
| Relevance | Answers the question | User feedback |
| Groundedness | Based on sources | Automated check |
| Coherence | Well-structured | Rubric scoring |
| Safety | No harmful content | Content classifier |

### Deliverables
1. Quality metrics framework
2. Measurement methodology
3. Baseline measurements (current state)
