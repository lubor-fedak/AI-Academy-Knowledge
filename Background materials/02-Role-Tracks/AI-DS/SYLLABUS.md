# AI Data Scientist (AI-DS) Track Syllabus

## Role Overview

The AI Data Scientist focuses on **model evaluation, experimentation, and quality assurance** for AI systems.

### Core Responsibilities
- Evaluate model performance and quality
- Design and run experiments
- Detect and mitigate bias
- Build evaluation frameworks
