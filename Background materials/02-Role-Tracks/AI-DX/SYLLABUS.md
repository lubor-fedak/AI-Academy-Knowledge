# AI Design & UX (AI-DX) Track Syllabus

## Track / Capability Overview

The AI-DX track strengthens **human-centered AI design skills** for participants in FDE, AI-FE and AI-PM.  
It focuses on research, prototyping and experience design for AI systems, and is treated as a **cross-cutting skill track**, not a standalone Designer job role in the Consult model.

### Core Skill Areas
- User research and journey mapping
- AI interaction design
- Prototyping and testing
- Design system creation
