# Day 10: Present Your Design

## AI-DX Track - Week 2, Day 10

> Design & UX skill track supporting FDE, AI-FE and AI-PM (not a separate Designer job role in the Consult model).

### The Situation: "Defend Your Decisions"

Present your AI experience design to stakeholders.
Every design choice should have a reason backed by user insight.

**Challenge:** Present and defend your design work.

### Presentation Structure
1. User research insights
2. Journey and pain points
3. Design solutions
4. Prototype demo
5. User testing findings
6. Next steps

### Week 2 Checkpoint
- Complete journey map
- Tested prototype
- Failure state designs
- Design system components
- Presentation with rationale
