# Day 10: Pitch Day

## AI-PM Track - Week 2, Day 10

### The Situation: "Sell the Vision"

Present your complete AI product strategy:
- Problem and opportunity
- Solution and MVP
- Business case and ROI
- Roadmap and next steps

1. The Problem (2 min)
2. The Solution (3 min)
3. The Business Case (3 min)
4. The Plan (3 min)
5. The Ask (2 min)
6. Q&A (5 min)
