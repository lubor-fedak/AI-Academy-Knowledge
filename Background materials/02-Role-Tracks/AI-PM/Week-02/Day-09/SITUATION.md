# Day 9: The Roadmap

## AI-PM Track - Week 2, Day 9

### The Situation: "What's Next After Launch?"

MVP is defined. But the board wants to see a 12-month roadmap.
How do you plan for a technology that changes every month?

**Challenge:** Create a flexible roadmap that provides direction without false precision.

### Roadmap Principles
- Near-term: High confidence, detailed
- Mid-term: Themes and goals, flexible
- Long-term: Vision and direction, adaptable

### Deliverables
1. 90-day detailed plan
2. 6-month themed roadmap
3. 12-month vision statement
