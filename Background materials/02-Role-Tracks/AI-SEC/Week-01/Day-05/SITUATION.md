# Day 5: First Blood

## AI-SEC Track - Week 1, Day 5

### The Situation: "Break This Chatbot"

Here's a demo chatbot. Your job: break it.
Find as many vulnerabilities as you can in 60 minutes.

**Challenge:** Red team an AI system and document findings.

### Attack Categories to Try
- Prompt injection (direct and indirect)
- Jailbreaking attempts
- Data exfiltration probes
- Privilege escalation
- Denial of service

### Deliverables
1. Vulnerability report (findings + severity)
2. Proof-of-concept for top 3 vulnerabilities
3. Recommended mitigations

### Week 1 Checkpoint
- Complete threat model
- Red team findings report
- Security recommendation presentation
