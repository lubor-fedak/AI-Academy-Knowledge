# AI Data Analyst (AI-DA) Track Syllabus

## Role Overview

The AI Data Analyst ensures AI projects have **data foundations, KPIs, and measurable impact**.

This track describes a data & analytics specialization within the Forward Deployed Engineer capability in the Consult role model.

### Core Responsibilities
- Build data pipelines for AI systems
- Define and track KPIs
- Create dashboards and reports
- Prove ROI with data
