# Day 6: Find the Baseline

## AI-DA Track - Week 2, Day 6

### The Situation: "Is 80% Good?"

Quality team reports: "AI accuracy is 80%."
Is that good? Bad? You need context.

**Challenge:** Establish baselines and benchmarks.

### Baseline Types

| Baseline | Example | Why It Matters |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Historical | Last month's performance | Track improvement |
| Target | Business requirement | Goal achievement |
| Industry | Competitor benchmarks | Competitive position |
| Human | Human performance | AI value proposition |

### Deliverables
1. Baseline documentation
2. Benchmark research
3. Contextualized metric report
