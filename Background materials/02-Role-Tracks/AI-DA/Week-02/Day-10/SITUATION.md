# Day 10: Present to Leadership

## AI-DA Track - Week 2, Day 10

### The Situation: "Board Meeting Next Week"

Your analytics will be presented to the board.
Make it crisp. Make it clear. Make it actionable.

**Challenge:** Create board-ready analytics presentation.

### Executive Presentation Rules
- Lead with insight, not data
- One message per slide
- Visual > table > text
- Always answer "so what?"
- End with recommendation

### Week 2 Checkpoint
- Complete analytics dashboard
- ROI calculation with evidence
- Data story presentation
- Forecasting model
- Board-ready summary
