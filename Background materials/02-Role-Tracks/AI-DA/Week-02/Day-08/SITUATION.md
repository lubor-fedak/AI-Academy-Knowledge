# Day 8: Tell the Story

## AI-DA Track - Week 2, Day 8

### The Situation: "Data Dump Doesn't Work"

You sent a 50-slide analytics report.
Nobody read it.

**Challenge:** Tell a compelling story with data.

### Data Storytelling Structure
1. **Hook:** The surprising insight
2. **Context:** Why it matters
3. **Evidence:** The supporting data
4. **So What:** The implication
5. **Now What:** The recommendation

### Deliverables
1. 5-slide story (not report)
2. One clear headline
3. One clear recommendation
