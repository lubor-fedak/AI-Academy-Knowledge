# Day 9: Predictive Analytics

## AI-DA Track - Week 2, Day 9

### The Situation: "What's Going to Happen?"

Leadership asks: "If current trends continue, what do we expect?"
They want predictions, not just reports.

**Challenge:** Build predictive models for AI system metrics.

### Predictive Use Cases
- Usage forecasting (capacity planning)
- Cost projection (budget planning)
- Quality trend (early warning)
- User growth (business planning)

### Deliverables
1. Forecasting model (simple)
2. Confidence intervals
3. Scenario analysis (best/base/worst)
