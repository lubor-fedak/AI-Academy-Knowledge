# Day 5: Build the Dashboard

## AI-DA Track - Week 1, Day 5

### The Situation: "I Need a Dashboard"

Leadership wants to see AI health at a glance.
One dashboard. Key metrics. Updated automatically.

**Challenge:** Create an executive dashboard for AI analytics.

### Dashboard Design Principles
- Most important number: BIG
- Trend: Is it getting better/worse?
- Comparison: vs. target, vs. last period
- Action: What does this mean?

### Week 1 Checkpoint
- Metrics framework
- Data pipeline (working)
- Executive dashboard
- Documentation of what metrics mean
