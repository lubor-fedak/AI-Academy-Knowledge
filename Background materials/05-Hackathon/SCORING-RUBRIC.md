# Hackathon Scoring Rubric

## Total: 100 points

---

## 1. Business Value (25 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 25 | Compelling problem, clear ROI, immediate value |
| 20 | Good problem fit, reasonable ROI case |
| 15 | Problem exists, value proposition unclear |
| 10 | Weak connection to stated problem |
| 5 | Solution looking for a problem |

**Questions judges ask:**
- Would a real customer pay for this?
- Is the ROI quantified and believable?
- Does it solve the right problem?

---

## 2. Technical Excellence (25 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 25 | Elegant architecture, clean code, robust |
| 20 | Good architecture, mostly clean, works well |
| 15 | Functional, some technical debt |
| 10 | Works but fragile |
| 5 | Barely functional |

**Questions judges ask:**
- Is the architecture appropriate?
- Could this scale to production?
- Is error handling comprehensive?

---

## 3. Innovation (20 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 20 | Novel approach, creative use of AI patterns |
| 15 | Some creative elements, good application |
| 10 | Standard approach, competent execution |
| 5 | Obvious solution, no innovation |

**Questions judges ask:**
- Did they try something new?
- Is this more than a chatbot wrapper?
- Creative problem-solving?

---

## 4. Presentation (15 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | Compelling story, flawless demo, engaging |
| 12 | Good story, demo worked, professional |
| 9 | Adequate presentation, minor demo issues |
| 6 | Unclear story, significant demo problems |
| 3 | Poor presentation, demo failed |

**Questions judges ask:**
- Did I understand the value proposition?
- Did the demo work?
- Would I want to see more?

---

## 5. Feasibility (15 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 15 | Ready for production path, clear roadmap |
| 12 | Feasible with reasonable effort |
| 9 | Possible but significant work needed |
| 6 | Major obstacles to production |
| 3 | Not feasible in current form |

**Questions judges ask:**
- Could this go to production in 3 months?
- Are the technology choices sustainable?
- Is the team honest about limitations?

---

## Bonus Points (up to 10)

- **Security Excellence:** +5 if AI-SEC checklist complete
- **Accessibility:** +3 if WCAG considerations shown
- **Documentation:** +2 if thorough documentation
