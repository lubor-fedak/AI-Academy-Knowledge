This package contains all content for the AI Academy 2026, structured around the **Forward Deployed Engineer capability** and related roles, implementing a **T-Shape learning model**.

**T-Shape Model:**
- **Horizontal Bar (Days 1-4):** Common foundations + Role Expo - all participants see all roles
- **Vertical Stem (Days 5-25):** Deep specialization in chosen role

2. Complete Role Expo on Day 4 to choose specialization
