# Mentor Quick Reference Card

## Session Structure (90 min)
| Phase | Time | Your Role |
|-------|------|-----------|
| Micro-Context | 5 min | Brief intro, vocab, 1 hint |
| Situation Launch | 5 min | Read challenge, clarify |
| Exploration | 50 min | Observe, minimal intervene |
| Debrief | 15 min | Facilitate discussion |
| Peer Exchange | 15 min | Pair students, listen |

## Intervention Triggers
| Signal | Wait | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| No progress | 15 min | Guiding question |
| Major wrong direction | 10 min | Direct redirect |
| Frustration | Now | Break down problem |
| Finished fast | Now | Challenge edge case |

## Key Phrases
✅ "What have you tried?"
✅ "What did AI Tutor say?"
✅ "What would happen if...?"
❌ "The answer is..."
❌ "You should do X"
❌ "Let me show you"

## Emergency Contacts
- Student crisis → HR Partner
- Tech failure → IT Support
- Content issues → Academy Lead
